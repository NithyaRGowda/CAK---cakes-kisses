About Us

CAK – Cakes & Kisses. We are Curators of Cakes, creating the most delicious cake occasions and experiences. We want to bring to you our select range of cakes for every occasion at an affordable price. We want our Cakes & Kisses on your plate whenever you want to have a warm piece of cake on a rainy day or lick your dessert spoon after a nice meal, treat yourself or someone special and for those special and memorable events like Birthdays, Weddings and anniversaries and festivals.

We would love to make someone’s day special at NGO’s, centres for the specially abled and outreach communities through our sponsoring. We have made our humble beginning this Christmas season, year 2020, offering the moist and melt-in-mouth Carrot Cakes, Plum Cakes (without alcohol) and Meringue Kisses.

We undertake multi-orders, single and bulk orders up to 30kg of cakes in a day. Bulk orders need to be placed 5 days in advance and we need 2 days prior notice of the other cakes. We deliver across Bengaluru city.

Look out for the Valentine’s season for a wider range of delectable cakes and bakes which will warm the hearts of your loved ones!

We are open to partnering with suitable home bakers, bakeries and enterprises for supplies and deliveries to bake a larger menu and create a wholesome experience to our customers.
